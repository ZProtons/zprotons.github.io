Frequently asked questions

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Reach out to our customer support team.

How does the website work?
Our website connects buyers with volunteers who can purchase and ship items from different countries. The buyer places an order from a specific website, and a volunteer located in that country will pick up the goods and mail them to the buyer's address.
Who are the volunteers?
Volunteers are individuals located in different countries who are willing to help buyers by purchasing and shipping items that are not available in the buyer's country. They receive a commission or cut from the profit for their services.
How can I become a volunteer?
If you are interested in becoming a volunteer, please contact us through the website. We will provide you with more information and guide you through the process.
What websites can I order from?
You can place orders from various websites, including popular online marketplaces like Amazon. However, the availability of items may vary depending on the country you are ordering from.
How much does the service cost?
The cost of the service includes the price of the item, any applicable taxes or customs fees, and the commission for the volunteer. The total cost will be provided to you before confirming your order.
